# Task 1 – Digital Literacy Presentation

## Overview
This task focuses on creating a presentation to demonstrate understanding of digital literacy concepts. The presentation was designed using an online tool Canva and covers key topics related to responsible and effective use of digital platforms.

## Topics Covered
- Introduction to Digital Literacy  
- Importance of Digital Skills in Modern Life  
- Safe Internet Practices  
- Responsible Use of Social Media  
- Awareness of Online Threats  

## Tools Used
- Canva (for presentation creation)

## Description
The presentation includes visually engaging slide with clear explanations of digital literacy concepts. It aims to educate users about safe and responsible behavior online while improving their understanding of digital tools.

## Learning Outcome
- Improved understanding of digital literacy  
- Awareness of online safety and responsible usage  
- Ability to create and present digital content effectively
