# Stay Safe Online: Cybercrime Prevention Checklist

- Never share your UPI PIN, OTP, or banking details with anyone.
- Do not click on unknown links or messages received via SMS, WhatsApp, or email.
- Always verify the identity of callers claiming to be from banks or customer support.
- Avoid installing apps from unknown or untrusted sources.
- Do not accept random payment requests without checking the sender.
- Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) on all important accounts.
- Regularly check your bank statements and transaction history.
- Use strong and unique passwords for different platforms.

**UPI & Financial Safety Tips:**
- Remember: You do NOT need to enter your UPI PIN to receive money.
- Never approve collect/payment requests from unknown people.

**Report Cybercrime:**
- National Cyber Crime Portal: https://cybercrime.gov.in
- Helpline Number: 1930
