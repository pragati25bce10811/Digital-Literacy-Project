# Task 5: Cybercrime Awareness Case Study & Prevention Guide

## Overview
This task focuses on understanding cybercrime and spreading awareness about online safety. It includes a case study of a cybercrime scenario and a prevention checklist to help users stay safe online.

## Platforms Used
- Internet (for research)
- National Cyber Crime Portal (cybercrime.gov.in)

## Work Done
I researched a cybercrime case related to UPI/online payment fraud and wrote a detailed case study explaining how the crime happens, who is targeted, and its consequences. I also created a prevention checklist with practical tips to help users avoid such cyber threats and included official reporting methods.

## Purpose of Task
- To understand different types of cybercrimes
- To spread awareness about online safety
- To learn preventive measures against cyber threats

## Learning Outcomes
- Gained knowledge about cybercrime methods and risks
- Learned how to stay safe while using online platforms
- Understood the importance of reporting cybercrimes
- Developed awareness about digital security practices
